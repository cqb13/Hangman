How To Play

- Enter a letter in the input field at the bottom of the screan.

- Press the guess button or the enter key on your keyboard to submit your guess.

- If your guess is in the word, it will replace a dashed line in the hidden word.

- If your guess is not in the word, you will loose a life. You only have 12 lives.
